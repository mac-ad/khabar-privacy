Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Khabar, a news aggregator application for Android. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Khabar is a simple news aggregator that provides you with news feeds from various sources that you select from our provided list. We do not create or publish news content ourselves; we only aggregate content from third-party sources.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information
  • Usage Data: Which news sources you select, articles you read, app usage patterns, and interaction with content
  • Preferences: Your chosen news sources, notification settings, and app configuration
  • Cache Data: Temporarily stored news content for offline viewing

Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • We do not require or collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Delivering news feeds from your selected sources
  • Remembering your preferred news sources and app settings
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Providing offline access to cached news articles
  • Analyzing app usage to fix bugs and improve features
  • Optimizing content delivery and loading times

Third-Party News Sources

Khabar aggregates news content from various third-party news sources. When you access news articles through our app, you may be directed to the original source's website or content may be displayed within the app. These third-party sources have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

The news sources may collect their own data when you view their content. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of the news sources you choose to follow.

Data Storage and Security

Your preferences and cached news content are stored locally on your Android device. We do not transmit your personal preferences or usage data to external servers except for the purpose of fetching news feeds from the sources you have selected.

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data, but please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Permissions Required

The Khabar app requires the following Android permissions:

  • Internet Access: Required to fetch news feeds from selected sources
  • Network State: To check internet connectivity before fetching content
  • Storage: To cache news articles for offline reading (if applicable)

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Choose which news sources to follow or unfollow at any time
  • Clear cached data through the app settings or Android system settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time, which will remove all locally stored data
  • Control notification preferences within the app

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
